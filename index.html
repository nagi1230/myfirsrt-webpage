The four doors of Gurudwara Sahib known as:

– Door of Peace

– Door of Livelihood

– Door of Learning

– Door of Grace

These doors are a symbol that people from all four points of the compass are welcome, and that members of all four castes are equally welcome.>

There’s always a light on in a Gurudwara, to show that the Guru’s Light is always visible and is accessible to everyone at any time.>
